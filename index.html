My Process —
Start to Finish

01
Discovery Call

We jump on a quick call to understand your vision, goals, target audience, and deadline. I ask the right questions so nothing is left ambiguous.

⏱ ~30 minutes
02
Footage & Brief Review

You send raw footage, script, references, and brand assets. I review everything and confirm the timeline before touching a single frame.

⏱ 24 hours
03
First Edit Draft

I deliver the first cut — rough assembly with structure, pacing, and music so you can see the story taking shape before polish is applied.

⏱ 2–3 business days
